here's what you can do: 
             
the prefix is '>' or '<'
every command has 3s cooldown
(keep in mind that this site will be updated constantly when new commands are being added)

general:
>help = displays this message
>ping = see if i'm up
>uptime = see how long i've been awake

fun:
>roll [optional:limit] = i roll the dice. if no limit 
                         is set, the default limit is 100

>coin = i flip a coin. it's either heads or tails!

>rps [choice] = play rock, paper, scissors with me! 
                pick yours and see who wins...

>joke = i tell you a bad joke

tools:
>binary [number] = i'm gonna convert your number to binary 
>hex [number] = i'm gonna convert your number to hexadecimal

animals:
>dog = get a random dog image and fact!
>cat = get a random cat image and fact!
>bird = get a random bird image and fact!
>fox = get a random fox image and fact!
>koala = get a random koala image and fact!
>panda = get a random panda image and fact!
>redpanda = get a random redpanda image and fact!
